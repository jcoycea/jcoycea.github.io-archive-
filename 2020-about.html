Joyce Aquino
Visual Designer

I’m Joyce, a visual designer based in Vancouver, BC with a keen interest in experience and interaction design, branding, and art direction. I strive to create compelling and purpose-driven designs using the principles of Enduring Design and Sustainable Design that provoke memorable interactions and inspire positive change.

Currently working as a Creative Lead at TEALEAVES and an interaction design graduate from the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

I work in the fields of visual design, interface and interaction design, user experience, illustration, print, packaging, web design, and branding.
